Why Choose Adin Bricks?

Adin brick has been providing architects, contractors, builders, landscapers and homeowners quality products and services for more than three decades. That’s because professionals know that start to finish, their projects are only as good as the materials they use to build them.

For this reason, we provide only the finest quality brick, stone, landscape, masonry and building supplies in the Balikpapan area for the entire scope of both residential and commercial projects. And that’s why Adin Brick Company is where your building begins.

Our Products

Brick

Our vast selection of materials include face, thin and structural brick along with special shapes, murals and sculptures to give your project the distinctive elegance that is the hallmark of great building and design.

Stone

For design flexibility, our vast assortment of stone choices include—natural stone in organic shapes and colors; manufactured stone for shape and color uniformity; architectural cast stone for consistent quality; limestone for long-lasting beauty and to add the ideal finishing touch to any building, personalized address stones.

Landscape

More than just bricks and stone, Adin Brick is one of the largest suppliers of landscape and masonry materials in Balikpapan. Our selection of unique landscaping choices and outdoor project solutions help contractors, landscapers and homeowners realize their ideas through from start to finish.

Block

Concrete block is a practical, long-lasting and cost-effective material used in building. Its strength, durability and superior fire rating make block an excellent building material alternative. Our block offering includes CMU, split face, half high, burnished and glazed.
